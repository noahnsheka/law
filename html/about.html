About Kigozi & Partners

East Africa's Premier Legal Firm

Our Story

Kigozi & Partners was established in 2008 by Principal Partner Christopher Kigozi, a seasoned legal professional with over 20 years of experience in corporate and commercial law. What began as a boutique practice serving a select clientele has evolved into one of East Africa's most respected legal firms, with a team of over 50 legal professionals.

Our growth is built on a foundation of integrity, excellence, and unwavering commitment to our clients' interests. We have successfully handled hundreds of transactions and litigation matters worth millions in value, earning the trust of blue-chip corporations, financial institutions, and government entities across the region.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional legal services that empower our clients to achieve their business objectives, navigate complex legal challenges, and build sustainable enterprises. We believe in being more than just advisors—we are strategic partners invested in our clients' success.
