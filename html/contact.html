Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an initial consultation take?

Initial consultations typically last 30-60 minutes. This gives us time to understand your situation and provide preliminary guidance on your legal options.

What are your fees?

We offer transparent billing with various fee arrangements including hourly rates, fixed fees, and contingency arrangements depending on the matter type.

How quickly can we get started?

We prioritize urgent matters and can typically begin work within 24-48 hours of retaining our services.

Do you handle cases outside Uganda?

Yes, we have partnerships and experience with legal matters across East Africa including Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

What is your communication approach?

Each client is assigned a dedicated attorney who provides regular updates via phone, email, or in-person meetings as preferred.

Is our information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All client information is protected by attorney-client privilege and our strict confidentiality protocols.
